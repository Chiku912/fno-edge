HOW TO USE
🎯 ATM (Impulsive) — At-the-money option. Delta ~0.50. Moves almost 1:1 with stock. Best for catching sharp, immediate moves.

💡 OTM (Profit Max) — Out-of-the-money option (~1.5–2% away). Lower premium (60–70% cheaper than ATM). If the target strike is hit, returns can be 3–8× the invested premium. Requires bigger price move.

Both update live as stock price changes — nearest strike and OTM target are always recalculated.
IMPACT FLAGS
⚡ CRITICAL Earnings, M&A, SEBI action, RBI policy. Expected move >3%.
◈ MEDIUM Rating change, block deal, FII flow, regulatory filing. 1–3% move.
● REGULAR AGM notice, SAST disclosure, minor insider trade. Neutral impact.
